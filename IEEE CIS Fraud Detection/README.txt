Project Title :-   IEEE-CIS Fraud Detection.

Technologies :-   Python along with some of its libraries like Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, 
                  Sklearn, SimpleImputer, SMOTE, RandomForrestClassifier.

Description :- 

Online transactions are one of the areas where fraud transactions can occur and can lead to loss of money.
Here we will detect online fraudulent transactions using machine learning techniques which will
improve the efficacy of fraudulent transaction alerts for millions of people around the world, 
helping hundreds of thousands of businesses reduce their fraud loss, increase their revenue and 
improve the customer experience.
